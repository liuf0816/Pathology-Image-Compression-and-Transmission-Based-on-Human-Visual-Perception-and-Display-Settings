Synopsis

This function may be used to remove some of the least recently used layers from the internal inactive list. Compositing layers are added to this list when remove_compositing_layer is called with a permanent argument of false. Old layers are also moved to the inactive list when set_frame is used to advance to a new frame in an animation or movie.

Arguments

max_inactive [int]

Number of most recently active compositing layers to leave on the inactive list.
